ANANDVAN COST & DONATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM - PROJECT REPORT

1. PROBLEM STATEMENT
Objective: To transition Anandvan from fragmented tracking methods to a centralized, web-based platform that streamlines budget allocation, expense monitoring, and donation tracking.

The Challenge:
- Lack of Centralization: Information across multiple initiatives was siloed, making it hard to get a holistic financial view.
- Transparency Gaps: Donors lacked real-time visibility into how their funds were being utilized.
- Inefficiency: Manual tracking delayed financial reporting and complicated decision-making for leadership.

---

2. THE SOLUTION: A UNIFIED MANAGEMENT ECOSYSTEM
The developed platform addresses these challenges through three primary pillars: Structure, Evidence, and Visibility.

A. Centralized Budget & Initiative Management
- Administrative Command Center: Admins can create and edit specific initiatives, allocating dedicated budgets for each.
- Visual Personalization: Initiatives can now be assigned custom cover images, making the platform more engaging for potential supporters.

B. Transparent Expense Tracking (Evidence-Based)
- Accountability: Staff members record expenses against specific initiatives.
- Receipt Verification: Every recorded expense can include a digital receipt upload, ensuring that every rupee spent is verifiable through "Proof of Expenditure."

C. Real-Time Donation Mapping
- Instant Allocation: Donations are linked directly to initiatives, allowing for real-time calculation of "Fund Availability" vs. "Budget Needed."
- Live Impact Statistics: The homepage features a live counter showing total funds raised globally, providing immediate social proof and motivation for donors.

---

3. KEY FUNCTIONAL RESULTS

Feature                          | Problem Solved
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Role-Based Access                | Ensures data integrity by separating Admin, Staff, and Donor functions.
Financial Transparency Report    | A public-facing ledger that shows every expense and its associated proof.
CSV Export                       | Allows for traditional accounting audits and off-line data analysis.
Automated Financial Charts       | Visualizes resource distribution (Budget vs. Raising vs. Spent).

---

4. IMPACT ON DECISION-MAKING
By digitizing these workflows, Anandvan leadership can now identify which initiatives are underfunded or over-budget in a single glance. More importantly, the system builds profound trust with donors by proving that their contributions are being "invested" directly into verified social causes.

Technology Stack: Flask, SQLAlchemy, CSV, Image/Receipt Handling.
